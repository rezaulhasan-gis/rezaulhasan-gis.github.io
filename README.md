Rezaul Hasan Bhuiyan is a GIS and remote sensing data analyst with expertise in machine learning, 3D modeling, and spatial data management. His researches interest lies in forest ecology, urban green space, and tree resilience and health, applying statistical and machine learning models to environmental analysis.
Rezaul’s academic journey includes an MSc in Geo-information Science and Earth Observation from the University of Twente and a BSc in Environmental Science from Noakhali Science and Technology University. He is skilled in geospatial programming (Python, R), GIS & remote sensing tools (QGIS, ArcGIS, SNAP,FME), and geospatial data management (PostGIS, Mysql), applying these technologies to develop data-driven solutions for environmental challenges.
